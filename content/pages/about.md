Here I write about things in software engineering that catch my attention. Most posts start as a way to think through a topic more clearly, but hopefully they end up being useful to others too.

I tend to stay with topics that have a bit more depth to them. I enjoy working on problems that are complex or unconventional, especially when they call for careful thinking, clear structure, and attention to detail.

Most of my work is somewhere between product engineering, infrastructure, and developer experience. I like systems that stay understandable under pressure and interfaces that feel deliberate instead of accidental.

This site is also a working project in its own right. It is where I keep technical notes, document architecture decisions, and leave myself enough context to come back later without having to rediscover everything from scratch.
